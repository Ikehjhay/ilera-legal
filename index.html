Privacy Policy

Ilera AI Business Platform — Last updated: July 15, 2026

Ilera ("we," "us," or "our") provides an AI-powered business automation platform that businesses use to communicate with their own customers over WhatsApp and other channels. This policy explains what information we collect, how it's used, and the choices available to both the businesses who use our platform ("Business Customers") and the people who message those businesses ("End Users").

Who this policy covers

This policy applies to two groups of people, and it's worth being clear about the difference:

  • Business Customers — companies that sign up to use Ilera to run AI-assisted customer support, ordering, and booking on their own WhatsApp number.
  • End Users — the customers of those businesses, who message a Business Customer's WhatsApp number and interact with the AI assistant on their behalf.

How we use this information

  • To operate the AI assistant: understanding a message, looking up a Business Customer's catalog, creating and tracking orders, and replying on WhatsApp.
  • To route order and delivery details to the Business Customer so they can fulfill an order.
  • To let a Business Customer view their own conversation and order history through their dashboard.
  • To maintain and improve the reliability and safety of the platform.
  • To meet legal, regulatory, and security obligations.

We do not sell personal information, and we do not use End User conversation content to train AI models unrelated to operating this platform.

Who we share information with

Operating this platform requires working with a small number of service providers, each acting as a data processor on our behalf:

  • Meta / WhatsApp Business Platform — to send and receive messages.
  • The AI model provider selected by each Business Customer — one of Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, DeepSeek, or Groq, depending on that business's configuration — to generate responses to End User messages.
  • Database and hosting providers (including MongoDB Atlas and our application hosting provider) — to store and run the platform.
  • Payment processors (such as Paystack or Flutterwave), where a Business Customer has enabled online payment.

Each of these providers is contractually restricted to using data only to provide their service to us, not for their own independent purposes.

Data retention

We retain conversation and order data for as long as a Business Customer's account is active, and for a reasonable period afterward to meet legal, accounting, and dispute-resolution needs. A Business Customer can request deletion of their account data at any time; an End User can request deletion of their own data by contacting the Business Customer they interacted with, or us directly at the email below.

Your rights

Depending on where you're located, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. This includes rights available under Nigeria's Data Protection Act (2023) and, where applicable, other regional data protection laws. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Children's privacy

This platform is intended for use by businesses and their adult customers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and a Business Customer is responsible for ensuring their own use of the platform complies with applicable rules regarding minors.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect information, including encryption in transit and restricted access to production systems. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, and we continually work to improve our protections.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the platform evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
